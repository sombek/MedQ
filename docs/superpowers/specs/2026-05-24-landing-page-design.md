# MedQ Landing Page Design

## Overview
The current landing page incorrectly positions MedQ as a "Medical Queue" management system. This redesign completely revamps the landing page to reflect MedQ's true purpose: a Medical Question Bank for medical students. The design focuses on a hybrid approach, combining exam preparation with clinical knowledge mastery.

## 1. SEO Meta Tags
- **Title**: MedQ | Medical Question Bank for Students
- **Description**: Master medical concepts and ace your exams with MedQ. Practice high-yield questions with detailed explanations.

## 2. Hero Section
- **Badge**: "For Medical Students"
- **Title**: "Master Medicine. Ace Your Exams."
- **Subtitle**: "The smartest question bank to test your knowledge, review detailed explanations, and prepare for clinical practice."
- **CTA**: "Start Practicing" (Links to the practice area or login)
- **Image**: Replace the current hospital reception image with an image of medical students studying or a clean mockup of the app interface.

## 3. How it Works Section (New)
A minimal, 3-step section explaining the core loop of the app:
- **Step 1: Choose a Specialty**
  - Description: Select from various medical fields to focus your study session.
- **Step 2: Test Your Knowledge**
  - Description: Answer high-yield questions designed to challenge your understanding.
- **Step 3: Learn & Improve**
  - Description: Review detailed explanations for every answer and track your progress over time.
